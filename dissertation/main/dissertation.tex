%   WHAT other places surround it (co-occurrence),

%

%

%   (41% of all mentions in the Leith & North sector), while
%   Walter Scott clusters around Old Town (33.5%).

%   Only a purpose-built visualisation can reveal this divergence.

\section{Problem Statement}
